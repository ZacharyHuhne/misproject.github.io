My Hobbies

What I enjoy doing outside of school.

Photography

My passion for photography started when I was in community college and I took a black and white film photography class. Since then, I have been out and about shooting photos of architecture, nature, cars, wildlife, and so much more. Photography is important to me because it allows me to express myself and show other people the little details in life that I find interesting. Here are some of my favorite shots!

Fishing

Fishing is one of my favorite things to do on my off days. Being out on the water by itself is very calming, and the excitment of having a fish on the line is the cherry on top. I enjoy fishing on the lake during the summer, but I also love to ice fish.

Weightlifting

On my free time, I love to lift weights in the gym. Going to the gym everyday gives me a sense of satisfaction and accomplishment. One of my goals is to be able to bench press 225 pounds. I work hard every day to try to make that happen.
